# Copy-Paste Hook Formulas
> Ready to use. Fill in the brackets.

## The Big 3 (Most Consistently Viral in 2026)

### 1. Contrarian Claim
```
"Stop [common action]. Here's why."
```
- "Stop paying for a website. Here's why."
- "Stop posting on social media if your website looks like this."
- "Stop paying $5,000 for a website."

### 2. Mistake Warning
```
"[Number] mistakes killing your [audience's thing]."
```
- "3 mistakes killing your local business online."
- "5 reasons your website isn't getting you customers."
- "The #1 mistake local business owners make with their website."

### 3. List Tease
```
"[Number] [things] that [impressive outcome]."
```
- "5 AI tools that replace a $5K/month marketing team."
- "3 things every local business website needs in 2026."
- "4 website fixes that doubled this plumber's phone calls."

## Niche-Specific Formulas

### The Caveman Hook (Nicholas Puruczky style)
```
"[Tool] just [killed/replaced/made obsolete] [thing]. Here's what to do now."
```
- "AI just killed $5,000 websites. Here's what smart business owners are doing instead."
- "Claude just replaced your marketing team. Here's the $0 version."

### The Credibility + Curiosity Hook
```
"I spent [time] in [field]. The real answer isn't [common answer] -- it's [truth]."
```
- "I've built 200 websites. The real reason businesses fail online isn't bad design. It's no phone number above the fold."

### The Comparison Hook
```
"[Cheap option] vs [your option]. Here's the difference."
```
- "A $0 Wix template vs. a custom website. Look at the difference."
- "$200/mo receptionist vs. $397/mo AI. Here's what happens at 2 AM."

### The DM Trigger Hook
```
"Comment '[word]' and I'll [valuable action] for free."
```
- "Comment 'AUDIT' and I'll review your website for free. No catch."
- "Comment 'DEMO' and I'll build you a free website mockup."

### The Transformation Hook
```
"I went from [bad] to [good] in [time] using [thing]."
```
- "I went from 0 to 14 clients in 90 days using AI agents."
- "This barbershop went from 3 to 15 calls a week. One website change."

### The "Did You Know" Hook
```
"Did you know [surprising stat]?"
```
- "Did you know 46% of Google searches are looking for a local business? And 56% of local businesses don't even have a website?"
- "Did you know 97% of people check your website before calling?"
