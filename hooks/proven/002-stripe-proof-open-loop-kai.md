## The Hook (First 5 Seconds)
> "Okay, I run a $319,000 a month website design agency and in this video I'm going to show you exactly how I got my first 20 to 25 clients cold calling."

## Why It Works
- **Psychology:** SHOW, don't tell. The Stripe dashboard with $319K MRR and $1.97M net volume is on screen before the first sentence finishes. The brain registers "this is real, not a claim" in under 2 seconds. Then the open loop fires with a specific promise ("exactly how I got my first 20-25 clients").
- **Pattern:** Stat hook + "I'll show you exactly" open loop. The revenue proof removes skepticism before it forms.
- **Audience filter:** Anyone interested in web design agencies or cold calling. The $319K/mo number is aspirational but within reach for agency owners, not so high it feels fake.
