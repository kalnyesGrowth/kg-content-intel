# Contrarian + Proof Demand (Screen Scroll Montage)

## Source
- **Creator:** AI Chris Lee (YouTube)
- **Video:** "Everyone Builds 3D Websites with Claude Code. I Sell Them for $10K."
- **URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mhIAd5lVMag
- **Views:** 139,000
- **Category:** Contrarian + Stat

## The Hook (First 5 Seconds)
> "Everyone says you can build $10,000 websites with AI. Nobody shows you a single client who actually paid $10,000 for one. I'm going to build a website and show you the exact outreach messages that got me first three clients."

## Why It Works
- **Psychology:** Two-punch contrarian strike. Punch 1: "Everyone says" immediately calls out the viewer's entire feed. Every AI website video they've watched gets thrown under suspicion. Punch 2: "Nobody shows you a single client who actually paid" is the kill shot. It names the exact gap in every other video: they show the build, never the sale. The viewer has been wondering this themselves but nobody said it out loud. Then he fills the gap with a concrete promise: "show you the exact outreach messages that got me first three clients." Not theory, not motivation. The actual messages.
- **Pattern:** Contrarian + Proof Demand + Open Loop. He's not just criticizing the trend, he's offering the missing proof everyone else omits. "Takes 20 minutes to build, takes 20 messages to sell" adds the efficiency promise.
- **Audience filter:** People who've already watched AI website tutorials and are frustrated they never learned how to SELL. This repels pure hobbyists and attracts people who want to monetize. The $10K price point signals premium positioning.
