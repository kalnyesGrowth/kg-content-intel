**Note:** Exact voiceover derived from visual context. Estimated: "In the last year I've built [X] websites for local businesses. Here's how much I charged for each one."

## Why It Works
- **Psychology:** Three triggers stack in sequence. (1) Portfolio quantity: 8+ professional websites flooding the screen = instant credibility ("this person has done this many times"). (2) Revenue specificity: non-round dollar amounts ($2,855, $1,785) feel real, not fabricated. (3) Variety proof: each website is a different niche (pest control, dental, legal, window cleaning, landscaping, home care), proving versatility.
- **Pattern:** Credibility + Curiosity. The results come FIRST, then the "how" becomes the open loop. Follows the "Client Result" framework from video-frameworks.md.
- **Audience filter:** Anyone wanting to sell websites to local businesses sees themselves in this. The specific dollar amounts create desire ("I could charge $3,000-$6,500 per site"). Business owners scroll past. Only aspiring/active web designers stay.

## The Pattern: Portfolio Cascade + Revenue Reveal
This is a two-phase visual hook:
1. **Cascade phase (0-7s):** Build credibility through QUANTITY. Each website entering the frame adds one more piece of proof. The viewer thinks "this guy has built a LOT."
2. **Revenue phase (7-12s):** Lock attention through SPECIFICITY. Dollar amounts on each site answer the viewer's unspoken question: "but how much can I actually charge?"

This pattern is replicable for ANY service business. Replace websites with client logos, dashboards, before/afters. The key is the STACKING + REVEAL structure.
