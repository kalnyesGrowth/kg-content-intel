## The Hook (First 5 Seconds)
> "I'm going to give you over 10 years of web design knowledge in just 7 minutes. No fluff, just the good stuff. Are you ready? Let's go."

## Why It Works
- **Psychology:** Time compression creates irresistible value density. "10 years in 7 minutes" is a 750,000:1 compression ratio that the brain can't ignore. The viewer calculates: "Even if this is half as good as promised, 7 minutes is nothing." The "no fluff" disclaimer preempts the skepticism that such a compressed promise creates. "Are you ready? Let's go" adds urgency and forward momentum, making it feel like a countdown has started.
- **Pattern:** Stat hook variant (10 years / 7 minutes) combined with Open Loop. The extreme time contrast IS the hook, not the topic itself.
- **Audience filter:** Beginner to intermediate web designers who want fast, dense education. The "10 years of knowledge" framing attracts people who know they have knowledge gaps but don't want a 4-hour course.
