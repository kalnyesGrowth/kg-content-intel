## Why It Works
- **Psychology:** Triple-layer credibility attack. (1) He names the exact claim his audience has been consuming ("local businesses are a gold mine"), validating their exposure. (2) He immediately contradicts it ("I really don't think that they are"), creating a pattern interrupt. (3) Before skepticism kicks in, he drops his credential ($72K/mo AI agency) so the contrarian take has weight. The viewer thinks: "Wait, this guy actually does what the gurus claim to teach, and he says it's wrong?"
- **Pattern:** Confession/Vulnerability + Contrarian. Anti-guru formula: attack the consensus, then prove you have standing to do so.
- **Audience filter:** People already consuming "sell AI to local businesses" content. Filters for experienced builders who suspect the guru advice is off, not fresh beginners.
