## Why It Works
- **Psychology:** The visual density of the n8n workflow graph (20+ connected nodes filling the screen) creates immediate "wow, that's complex, I need to learn this" curiosity. The impossibly broad claim ("does EVERYTHING, 100% automated") forces the viewer to stay to verify if it's real. He then immediately grounds the claim with specifics (calendar, email, CRM, meetings) within 6 seconds.
- **Pattern:** Caveman hook variant. Show the impressive system first, then explain. The visual IS the hook, not the words.
- **Audience filter:** Anyone interested in AI automation, no-code tools, productivity. The n8n interface signals "this is technical but achievable" to builders.
