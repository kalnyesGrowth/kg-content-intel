## Why It Works
- **Psychology:** Dual trigger. "Getting a ton of messages" = social proof (other people want this, so it must be valuable). "Five meetings a day" = specific open loop with a concrete, believable number that forces the viewer to stay and find out how.
- **Pattern:** Credibility + Curiosity hybrid from hook-formulas.md
- **Audience filter:** Anyone doing outbound sales or agency work self-selects in. The casual delivery repels polished-content seekers and attracts grinders who want real tactics.
