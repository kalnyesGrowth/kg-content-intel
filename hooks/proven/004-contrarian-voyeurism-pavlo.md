## The Hook (First 5 Seconds)
> "No one actually shows you how to get clients. Yes, they tell you about it, they give you the tactics and the strategies, but what about just sitting down and watching someone do it?"

## Why It Works
- **Psychology:** Three triggers stacked. (1) Contrarian accusation ("no one actually shows you") validates the viewer's frustration with all the fluff content they've consumed. (2) The us-vs-them framing puts Pavlo on the viewer's side against "gurus who just talk." (3) The promise is voyeuristic: not "I'll teach you" but "sit down and WATCH someone do it." 341K people clicked to be a fly on the wall during real cold calls.
- **Pattern:** Contrarian Strike from hook-library.md, combined with Process/Behind-the-scenes format.
- **Audience filter:** People who have already consumed "how to get clients" content and are frustrated it never shows the real thing. This hooks experienced agency builders, not beginners.

## Visual Breakdown
- **Frame 1 (0-1s):** Talking head, tight webcam crop. Bearded young man in dark gray t-shirt and black cap with gold logo. Looking directly into camera. Clean modern bedroom/office background with vertical wood slat accent wall.
- **Frame 2 (1-2s):** Same framing, no cuts, no transitions. Direct eye contact sustained. Expression shifts to frustration/emphasis on "shows you how to get."
- **Frame 3 (2-3s):** Still same continuous shot. Slightly wider eyes, leaning forward. Word "clients" lands with emphasis. No text overlay, no graphics, no intro animation.
- **Text overlay:** NONE for the first 5 seconds. Pure talking head.
- **Setting:** Modern apartment bedroom/office. Natural wood slat accent panel, gray tufted headboard/sofa. Cream/white walls. Natural daylight, no ring light.
- **Camera:** Webcam height, straight on, slightly above eye level. Tight crop from mid-chest up. Face fills ~60% of frame.
- **Audio:** Direct voiceover, no background music, no sound effects. Raw webcam mic. The lack of polish IS the production choice.
- **Bonus (35s):** Cuts to archival footage of himself as a teenager at a small dark desk (the "mom's basement origin"), holding a phone. Visual proof of starting from zero.
