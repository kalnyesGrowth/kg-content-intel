## Why It Works
- **Psychology:** Three-layer credibility stack. (1) Year timestamp ("2023") anchors a real timeline, not vague claims. (2) Cinematic B-roll montage (laptop work, whiteboard strategy, team collaboration at night) shows this person DOES the work, not just talks about it. (3) $5,000,000 revenue counter with actual analytics dashboards is the undeniable proof. The viewer thinks: "This guy made $5M doing what the title says. I need to watch."
- **Pattern:** Credibility + Stat hybrid. Opens with lifestyle proof, closes with the number. Follows the "Client Result" framework but applied to self.
- **Audience filter:** Anyone interested in AI, automation, or agency work self-selects hard. The production quality and $5M figure also filter OUT beginners looking for quick cash and filter IN serious builders.

## The Pattern: Cinematic Credibility Montage + Revenue Counter
This is a HIGH PRODUCTION three-phase hook:
1. **Anchor phase (0-1s):** Year timestamp grounds the story in reality
2. **Montage phase (2-4s):** Rapid B-roll cuts prove the person does real work (desk, whiteboard, team). Film grain adds documentary authenticity
3. **Revenue phase (9-10s):** Counter builds to $5M with dashboard proof surrounding the talking head

This pattern requires higher production investment than the other hooks in the library. The B-roll, film grain, animated text, and floating dashboards all need editing. But the payoff is massive: 3.3M views, the highest in our library by far.
