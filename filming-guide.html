Filming Quick Rules

⏱
2-Second Hook
First word IS the hook. No warmup. No "hey guys." 1.3s on TikTok, 2.1s on IG.
📷
1:1 Aspect Ratio
All content generated at 1:1. Never mix formats. Never convert from 4:3.
🔈
Captions Always
85% watch without sound. Burned-in captions = 25% higher completion rate. Bold sans-serif.
💪
Energy +20%
20% higher energy than normal conversation. Raw > polished. Human moments beat production.
💬
Market Language
"Phone calls" not "leads." "Customers" not "prospects." "Show up on Google" not "rank."
🎬
Batch Film
Film 3-5 videos in one session. Edit with captions + trending audio. Schedule throughout the week.
