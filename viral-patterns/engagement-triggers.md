# Engagement Triggers (2026)

## Signal Weights by Platform

| Signal | IG Reels | TikTok | YouTube Shorts | LinkedIn |
|--------|----------|--------|----------------|----------|
| DM Shares | HIGHEST | High | Medium | N/A |
| Saves | Very High | Very High | High | High |
| Quality Comments | High | High | High | Very High |
| Watch Time | High | HIGHEST | Very High | High (Depth Score) |
| Likes | Medium | Medium | Medium | Medium |

## How to Trigger Saves
- Checklists, frameworks, step-by-step processes
- "Save this for later" value content
- Example: "5 things every local business website needs in 2026. Save this."
- THE STACK framework designed specifically for saves

## How to Trigger Shares / DM Sends
- Create content people want to send to a friend
- Relatable pain points: "When the website your nephew built looks like this..."
- "Tag a business owner who needs this"
- DM sends are THE most heavily weighted signal for Reel distribution

## How to Trigger Quality Comments
- Sentence-length comments outrank single-word comments by far
- Diagnostic questions that require finishing the video to answer
- "What's the #1 thing stopping your business from getting more calls? Drop it below."
- AVOID: "YES/NO" polls, "Type 1 or 2" -- platforms actively suppress these

## Early Engagement Rules
- Reply to EVERY comment in the first hour
- Algorithm monitors early engagement depth
- TikTok shows to small test audience first. Performance determines wider push.
- Ignoring early comments = losing conversation thread ranking signal

## What NOT to Do
- "Comment NEED to get the link" -- Meta penalizing in 2026
- Engagement bait (polls, fake questions) -- platforms suppress
- Recycled content -- originality scoring active on IG and TikTok
- External links in captions -- LinkedIn penalizes by 60%
