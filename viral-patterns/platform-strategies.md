# Platform-Specific Strategies (2026)

## Instagram Reels
- **Algorithm priority:** Shares via DM > Saves > Comments > Likes
- **Originality Score:** Active. Reposted TikToks with watermarks = tanked reach
- **Optimal length:** 7-30s for viral. 30-90s for deeper storytelling with followers.
- **Hook window:** 3 seconds (some data says 1.7s)
- **Trending audio:** Use tracks with under 5,000 uses to catch trends before saturation
- **Frequency:** 3-4 Reels/week optimal. Mix with 2-3 carousels, 1-2 static posts.
- **Key rule:** Topic must be perfectly clear to both algorithm and viewer

### KG IG Strategy
- Post as REEL only (never static image)
- Higgsfield nano_banana_pro hook frame (1:1)
- Editorial slides compiled into video with background music
- Unique image per post, never recycled

## TikTok
- **Algorithm priority:** Watch time > view count
- **Hook window:** 1.3 seconds. Fastest platform.
- **Saves + shares** outweigh likes significantly
- **Search optimization:** 84% of searches happen in exploration phase. Keyword-optimized captions critical.
- **Niche advantage:** Community-aligned > random viral. Small niche accounts outperform large generic ones.
- **Originality:** Downranks low-effort reposts, watermarks, mass-produced videos

### KG TikTok Strategy
- Hook in first word. No warmup.
- Keyword-optimize for "web design", "AI agent", "local business", "website"
- Target specific business types (plumber, barber, restaurant) for niche community

## YouTube Shorts
- **Biggest advantage:** 80% of views from non-subscribers. Best top-of-funnel.
- **Engagement rate:** 5.91% average. Highest of all three platforms.
- **Search-first:** Algorithm surfaces helpful answers even from zero-follower channels
- **Length:** Up to 60 seconds. Clarity and helpfulness > production value.

### KG YouTube Strategy
- Longer educational content (30-60s)
- "How to" format optimized for search
- PASTOR framework works here for complex offers

## LinkedIn
- **Algorithm priority:** Dwell time + educational content
- **Penalizes:** Engagement bait (-60%), external links (-60%)
- **Depth Score:** New ranking signal measuring engagement duration
- **Video format:** Vertical 1080x1920 gets distribution boost. Under 90s.
- **Always add subtitles**
- **Surprise:** Multi-image posts have 3.71% engagement -- 2x video (1.80%). Carousels earn 2.4x more impressions.

### KG LinkedIn Strategy
- Unique Higgsfield image per post (not video)
- Educational/authority content
- No external links in post body (put in comments)
- Consider carousels for higher engagement

## Cross-Platform Rules
- Hook in 2 seconds max. No warmup. First word IS the hook.
- Energy 20% higher than normal conversation
- Raw > polished. Human moments beat production.
- Captions mandatory (85% watch without sound)
- Batch film 3-5 videos per week in one session
- Bold sans-serif text overlays in first 3 seconds = 14% lift in conversion clicks
