## Algorithm Rules

### What Gets Pushed
- High watch time (completion rate)
- DM shares (IG Reels)
- Saves (all platforms)
- Quality comments
- Original content
- Niche community alignment

### What Gets Suppressed
- Recycled content with watermarks
- Engagement bait ("Type 1 or 2")
- External links (LinkedIn)
- Transactional CTAs ("Comment NEED for the link" on Meta)
- Low-effort reposts
- Mass-produced content
